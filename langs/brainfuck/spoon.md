# Spoon

## Instructions

Spoon instructions that are isomorphic to Brainfuck instructions:

- `1` — `+`
- `000` — `-`
- `010` — `>`
- `011` — `<`
- `0011` — `]`
- `00100` — `[`
- `001010` — `.`
- `0010110` — `,`

Outputs the stack:

- `00101110` (`DEBUG`) — `#`

Quits the program:

- `00101111` (`EXIT`)
